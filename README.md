

# A Comprehensive .NET Core Business ERP & POS System for Streamlined Company Management

Take full control of your business operations with this all-in-one, web-based Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) solution. Built on the robust .NET Core framework with jQuery, this application integrates Point of Sale (POS), inventory, sales, purchases, and expenses into a single, seamless platform.

Go beyond basic management with deep, data-driven insights. The system provides extensive historical tracking and a suite of built-in reports for critical areas like stock levels, payment status, expense breakdowns, purchase orders, item barcodes, and employee attendance.

Key features include:
*   **Unified Business Hub:** Manage POS, inventory, sales, and purchases from one central dashboard.
*   **Real-Time Reporting & Analytics:** Make informed decisions with powerful reports on stock, finances, and workforce.
*   **Complete Historical Data:** Track every transaction and change with a comprehensive audit trail.
